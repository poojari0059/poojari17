                                                                    <b><h1><center>MIX-N-MATCH</center></h1></b>
   <b><h1>Calculator</h1></b>
<font face="arial">
<font size="4">
<p>
   Implement a calculator, which should be able to perform following type of operations.<br>
<ol>
 <li><b>ADD</b>:Addition of two numbers.</li>
<li><b>SUB</b>:Subtract two numbers.</li>
<li><b>MUL</b>:Multplication of two numbers.</li>
<li><b>DIV</b> :Division of two numbers(result should be floor  of first number/second number).</li>
<li><b>MAX</b>:Find maximum between two number,if both are equal result should be 0.</li>
<li><b>MIN</b>:Find minimum between two number,if both are equal result should be 0.</li>
<li><b>MOD</b>:modulus of numbers.</li>

</ol>
 
</p>

<b>Input Format</b><br>
First line of the input has an integer <b>Q</b>(Queries). <b>Q</b> cases follow. <br>
Each Query contains <b>type</b>(type of operation), <b>A</b>(first number) and <b>B</b>(second number) separated by space.<br><br> 

<font color="red">Constraints Updated!</font><br>
<b>Constraints</b> 
<ul>
<li>1<=<b>Q</b><=10<sup>2</sup></li>
<li>1<=<b>type</b><=7</li>
<li> -10<sup>3</sup><= <b>A,B</b> <=10<sup>3</sup></li>
<li>For DIV and MOD <b>B</b> wil not be 0</li>
</ul>
<b>Output Format</b><br>
For each test case, print the result in a new line.<br><br>

<b>Sample Input</b> <br>
 8<br>
1 2 4<br>
2 3 2<br>
3 2 4<br>
4 4 2<br>
4 3 2<br>
5 1 2<br>
6 2 3<br>
7 5 2<br>

<br>
<b>Sample output</b><br>
6<br>
1<br>
8<br>
2<br>
1<br>
2<br>
2<br>
1<br>
